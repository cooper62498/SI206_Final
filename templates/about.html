Thanks for checking out my project
This is my Final Project for SI 206: Data-Oriented Programming

I came up with this idea because skiing is one of my passions and I wanted to see how I could incorporate data from skiing into a Final Project

The Data

Open Snow

This website is run by Joel Gratz and a team of Meteorologists. They are professionals at forecasting snow amounts for skiing. From the wesbite, I scraped each state and province to get the snow forecast for the next 5 days. With those amounts, I took an average of the forecast and used that data within the chart.

Yelp

I used the Yelp Fusion API to pull the data of the rating of the ski area. While most places had ratings, some of the smaller places lacked a rating. Something cool with this, I used the Unicode stars on the map of the US and Canada to show the rating rather than giving a number. The only drawback with this is that Unicode does not have half star symbols so I needed to round the ratings to the nearest start.
